Sir Venki Ramakrishnan

Nobel Laureate in Chemistry

Position: Group Leader, MRC Laboratory of Molecular Biology

Title: Honorary Professor of Structural Biology

Location: University of Cambridge

Background & Research: Sir Venki Ramakrishnan is a structural biologist who transitioned from physics to molecular biology. He is best known for his pioneering work on the ribosome - the molecular machine in cells that makes proteins - using X-ray crystallography.

Cambridge University's faculty represents the gold standard in academic excellence, with a legacy spanning over eight centuries.
