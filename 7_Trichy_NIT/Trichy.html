Dr. Ganesan Kannabiran

Senior Professor – Department of Management Studies

Biography

One of the most senior and respected professors at NIT Trichy with over 30 years of academic and administrative experience. He has served as Dean (Research & Consultancy), Director-in-Charge of NIT Trichy, and Founding Director of IIT Sri City.

Research Areas

Information Systems Innovation & Entrepreneurship Digital Transformation SME Development Strategic Management

Awards & Recognition

  • Recognized nationally for academic leadership
  • Member of multiple national academic bodies
  • Advisor for several government and industry committees

Publications

  • 150+ research papers in top-ranked journals
  • Published in management and information systems journals
  • Reviewer for international journals (Elsevier, Springer)
