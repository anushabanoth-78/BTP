Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur

Contexts Involving Faculty Members

The phrase "three professors from IIT Kanpur" appears in media reports in connection with two unrelated events - a significant caste-based harassment case from 2018 and a Teachers' Day Distinguished Award presentation in 2023. This page clarifies both contexts.

Context 1: Caste-Based Harassment Case (2018)

A significant and widely reported incident involving allegations of caste-based harassment against a Dalit assistant professor, Dr. Subrahmanyam Saderla.

Accused Professors

Although some reports mention "three professors," this case actually involved four faculty members:

Prof. Chandrashekhar Upadhyaya

Professor at IIT Kanpur

Prof. Sanjay Mittal

Professor at IIT Kanpur

Prof. Ishant Sharma

Professor at IIT Kanpur

Prof. Rajeev Shekhar

Former Professor at IIT Kanpur

Later became the Director of the Indian School of Mines, Dhanbad

Key Developments

  • Multiple internal inquiry committees at IIT Kanpur found the professors responsible for mental harassment, intimidation, and misconduct
  • Disciplinary actions included demotion for three professors and a strict warning to the fourth
  • An FIR was filed under the SC/ST (Prevention of Atrocities) Act and the Information Technology Act
  • The accused challenged the FIR in the Allahabad High Court, which stayed their arrest and later quashed the FIR
  • This High Court decision was later challenged in the Supreme Court, keeping the legal matter active

Summary: In 2018, four professors at IIT Kanpur were involved in a high-profile caste-based harassment case filed by a Dalit faculty member. Internal committees found them guilty of harassment and intimidation, leading to disciplinary actions and prolonged legal proceedings.

Context 2: Teachers' Day Distinguished Award (2023)

A recognition event often misinterpreted as being related to the "three professors case."

Purpose of the Award

The award recognized exceptional contributions in areas such as:

  • Student placements and career development
  • Startup ecosystem development
  • Research excellence

Note: The publicly accessible summaries do not list the three professors' names, which is why the event is often referenced only as "three professors from IIT Kanpur receiving the Teachers' Day award."

Summary: In 2023, three IIT Kanpur professors received a Teachers' Day Distinguished Award for outstanding contributions to placements, startup initiatives, and research. The exact names were not included in the initial public briefings.

General Information About IIT Kanpur Faculty

  • IIT Kanpur has over 570 faculty members across 20 academic departments

  • Individual profiles include research areas, publications, achievements, and contact details

Summary: IIT Kanpur is one of India's premier engineering institutes with a strong faculty body of over 570 professors spanning science, engineering, humanities, and interdisciplinary fields.
