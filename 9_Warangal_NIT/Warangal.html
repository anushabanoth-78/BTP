National Institute of Technology, Warangal

Prominent Faculty Members

The National Institute of Technology (NIT) Warangal is home to many accomplished faculty members well-known for their impactful teaching, research contributions, and leadership roles. Below are three notable professors, frequently recognized by students and academic circles for their expertise and influence.

These profiles are illustrative examples of faculty members recognized for their teaching excellence and research impact.

Prof. R. V. Chalam

Professor

Department of Mechanical Engineering

Specialization

Kinematics Dynamics Machine Design Theory of Machines Design of Machine Elements

Highlights

  • Highly respected for simplifying complex mechanical engineering concepts with exceptional clarity
  • Known for an engaging and concept-driven teaching style, making him extremely popular among students
  • Recognized for ability to combine theoretical foundations with practical engineering insight

Teaching Style: Concept-driven, engaging, and focused on practical applications

Prof. P. Bangaru Babu

Head, Design Engineering Section

Department of Mechanical Engineering

Expertise

Engineering Design CAD/CAM Optimization Product Design Curriculum Development

Highlights

  • Expert in Design Engineering with broad contributions to teaching and curriculum development
  • Played a key role in strengthening the Design Engineering Section through academic leadership
  • Actively involved in mechanical engineering education, guiding numerous student projects
  • Strong contribution to engineering education and research

Leadership: Heads the Design Engineering Section at NIT Warangal

Prof. G. V. S. Nageswara Rao

Professor

Department of Metallurgical and Materials Engineering

Specialization

Materials Science Metallurgy Structural Materials Advanced Materials High-temperature Materials

Highlights

  • Prolific researcher with numerous high-value projects from DRDO and DAE
  • Major research work includes advanced materials development and high-temperature materials
  • Expert in metallurgical characterization and processing of structural alloys
  • Well published in national and international journals
  • Recognized for impactful contributions to India's defence and atomic energy research

Funding Agencies: DRDO (Defence Research and Development Organisation), DAE (Department of Atomic Energy)
