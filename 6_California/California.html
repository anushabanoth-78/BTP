World Universities

Caltech collaborates with leading universities worldwide to advance scientific research and education. Our global partnerships include institutions across North America, Europe, Asia, and Australia.

Key partner universities include MIT, Stanford, Cambridge, Oxford, ETH Zurich, University of Tokyo, and many others. These collaborations enable student exchanges, joint research projects, and shared resources.

Through these international partnerships, Caltech maintains its position at the forefront of global scientific innovation and discovery.

Research Groups

Caltech hosts numerous cutting-edge research groups across various scientific disciplines. Our research groups are known for their innovative approaches and groundbreaking discoveries.

Major research areas include gravitational-wave astrophysics, quantum mechanics, polymer chemistry, bio-imaging, nanotechnology, and sustainable energy solutions.

Each research group provides opportunities for undergraduate and graduate students to participate in meaningful scientific exploration under the guidance of world-renowned faculty members.
